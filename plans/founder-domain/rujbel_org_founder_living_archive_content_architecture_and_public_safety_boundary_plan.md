- Do not publish private recovery/adversity details without dignity review.
- Do not overclaim professional authority.
- Do not imply official institutional backing unless verified.
- Do not imply meUus services are already operational unless verified.
- Do not claim awards/certifications unless evidence exists.
- Do not present AI support as human partnership/legal entity.
- Do not expose private emails, screenshots, payment/gift details, or account data beyond safe summary.
- Do not publish raw CV without public-safety rewrite.
- Do not publish private archive material directly.
- Do not treat personal story as proof of meUus institutional readiness.
- Do not use rujbel.org to bypass meUus governance, review, or evidence rules.

## 7. Evidence Labeling System

Every founder claim should carry one of these labels before public use:

- Verified: externally or documentarily confirmed and safe to cite publicly.
- Self-reported: stated by the Founder but not independently verified.
- Document-supported but not public: evidence exists but should not be publicly exposed.
- Publicly visible: already visible in a public source.
- Private / not for publication: evidence or claim must remain internal.
- Draft / needs review: not ready for public use.
- Future intention: intended direction, not current fact.

No founder claim should move to public copy without an evidence label and a public-safety review decision.
