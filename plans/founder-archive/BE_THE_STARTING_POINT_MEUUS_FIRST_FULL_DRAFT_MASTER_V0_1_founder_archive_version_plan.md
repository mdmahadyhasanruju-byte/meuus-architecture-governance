# Founder Archive Version Plan

## Governance Boundary

This plan does not create the Founder archive version.

This plan does not edit or rewrite the manuscript.

This plan does not approve publication.

This plan does not make private material canonical.

This plan does not make the manuscript Frozen.

This plan does not amend Frozen governance.

This plan only defines how Founder-private material should be separated, protected, and possibly abstracted later.

## Founder Archive Purpose

- Preserve Founder-origin material honestly.
- Protect Founder dignity.
- Protect family/private people.
- Protect recovery-sensitive material.
- Protect emotional/mental-health-sensitive material.
- Protect religious/spiritual vulnerability.
- Protect legal/professional-sensitive history.
- Prevent pain from becoming marketing.
- Prevent personal story from becoming proof.
- Preserve origin without public exposure.
- Allow future public manuscript to use abstracted principles safely.

## Archive Categories

### A. Founder Origin Material

Personal beginning, intention, responsibility, Client #001 framing.

### B. Founder Vulnerability Material

Pain, fear, shame, overload, exhaustion, emotional state.

### C. Family / Private-Person Material

Mother, father, brother, sister, private family memory, identifiable people.

### D. Addiction / Recovery-Sensitive Material

Drug/recovery/rehab-related references and adjacent themes.

### E. Emotional / Mental-Health-Sensitive Material

Mental state, instability, healing language, emotional distress.

### F. Religious / Spiritual Vulnerability Material

Faith, guilt, repentance, Quran/Seerah framing, spiritual responsibility.

### G. Legal / Professional-Sensitive Material

Disputes, legal responsibility, professional claims, legal hub adjacency.

### H. Raw Correction / Internal History

Screenshots, "abar daw", command mode pause, internal AI/founder correction moments.

### I. Future-System Overclaim Risk Material

AI, DLAS, Journey, Soul, paid levels, service availability, app/runtime claims.

### J. Public-Impact / Bangladesh-Scale Material

National transformation, future generations, social impact claims.

## Archive Treatment Labels

## Public Abstraction Rules

- Convert personal pain into principle only when necessary.
- Use "origin insight" instead of "proof."
- Do not quote family/private people publicly.
- Remove identifiable private-person details.
- Do not use addiction/recovery history as marketing.
- Do not imply treatment, diagnosis, legal advice, religious authority, AI authority, or DLAS authority.
- Use short, bounded reflections instead of raw private narrative.
- Preserve Islamic phrases respectfully, but do not turn spiritual language into authority.
- Separate Founder story from system validation.
- Separate Founder archive from public manuscript.
- Archive first when uncertain.

## Public Manuscript Separation Rule

Future public manuscript should prefer:

- doctrine
- principle
- architecture-safe truth
- Claim <= Evidence
- birth is not completion
- public foundation is not full platform
- prototype is not production
- research blueprint is not proof
- listening before knowledge
- no person should be reduced

Future public manuscript should avoid:

- raw private pain
- identifiable family/private details
- addiction/recovery specifics
- unverified operational claims
- future-system-as-live wording
- therapy/legal/religious authority wording
- personal story as system proof
