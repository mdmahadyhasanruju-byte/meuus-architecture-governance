## 5. Evidence/Status Concerns

- "Zero to Pro" could imply guaranteed fluency if not carefully labeled.
- "Certification" must be marked Future unless legally and operationally approved.
- Arabic/Quran/Seerah support must be educational and not religious ruling.
- Audio/pronunciation/AI feedback must be marked Placeholder/Future unless implemented.
- Paid/registered levels must not be presented as live unless backend/payment/account system exists.
- Vocabulary accuracy requires linguistic review.
- Arabic transliteration in Bangla script requires specialist review.
- Knowledge lesson content requires source/provenance review.
- Data/information/knowledge/wisdom claims require careful educational framing.
- "Professional fluency" must be framed as roadmap, not guarantee.
- Public pages need disclaimers and status labels.
