## 5. Allowed Communication During Hold

Only the following are allowed:

- Internal governance notes.
- Private review messages.
- Factual founder updates that say "under review".
- One-to-one trusted feedback requests.
- Evidence collection.
- Correction planning.
- Non-public testing.
- Private screenshots for review.
- Statements that explicitly say not launched / not complete / under governance review.

## 6. Not Allowed Communication During Hold

The following are prohibited:

- "meUus launched"
- "Phase One launched" as public product launch
- "meUus app is live"

- "rujbel.org is complete"
- "official production-ready platform"
- "verified curriculum"
- "legal/religious/psychological/medical service"
- Any claim that implies operational services beyond evidence
