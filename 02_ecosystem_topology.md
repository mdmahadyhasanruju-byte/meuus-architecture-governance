## Purpose

Develop a more detailed conceptual topology for the meUus ecosystem from the roles, boundaries, and relationships established in `01_master_architecture.md`.

This Draft identifies the intended work without defining the topology in full. It is exploratory, is not canonical, and must not be cited as authoritative.

---

## Scope

This document is intended to clarify:

- Principal ecosystem domains.
- Conceptual relationships among domains.
- Responsibility boundaries.
- Allowed and forbidden cross-domain movement.
- Interaction among governance, public institution, knowledge, and runtime.
- Conceptual movement of status and evidence.
- Separations that must be preserved.

The detailed topology remains to be developed in a later Working draft.
