## 3. Domain-by-Domain Findings

### A. `meuus.org`

- Intended role: Understand / public starting point.
- Observed current status: Public foundation exists and is reachable. The home page describes a "Live Public Foundation" and "Founding Phase" with a browser-local Journey prototype and clear not-live boundaries for full app, accounts, AI runtime, DLAS runtime, cloud storage, payments, rewards/subscriptions, and automated decisions.
- Current claims observed: Public foundation, browser-local Journey prototype, current status page, manual expression-of-interest path, initial privacy/terms/consent/appreciation/domain boundaries, and future ecosystem directions.
- Live/public/preview/future separation: Mostly clear. The page distinguishes public foundation and browser-local prototype from future app, backend, accounts, AI runtime, payments, rewards, subscriptions, and wider ecosystem services.
- Overclaim risks: The phrase "Live Public Foundation" is accurate in context but could be misunderstood as full launch if reused without nearby boundaries.
- Correction needs: Keep "public foundation" language close to "not full ecosystem completion" language. Ensure any entry links to app/account/payment-like experiences remain bounded by current-status warnings.
- Audit decision for this domain: Mostly aligned.

### B. `meuussoul.com`

- Intended role: Learn / source library / reflection / knowledge.
- Observed current status: Public site reachable and presenting a broad human-development ecosystem and content experience. It contains substantial book/journey/progress/founder/profile-style language and visible personal/contact details.
- Current claims observed: "Human Development Ecosystem," "Nine Books," "One Complete Journey," progress-style content, founder/client identity, address and email details, and learning modules including Seerah, English practice, emotional intelligence, and Bangladesh law basics.
- Language & Knowledge Hub relationship: Governance says Language & Knowledge Hub route work is merged and public-route-verified under the candidate public foundation while announcement remains held. `meuussoul.com` should align with Learn / source library / reflection / knowledge and avoid implying complete course, complete platform, or verified authority.
- Public-preview / review-bounded content status: Needs stronger visible status labels. Some sections look like active product/course/dashboard content rather than draft or review-bounded learning/source material.
- Religious/legal/professional authority risks: Medium to high. References to Seerah and Bangladesh law basics require clear non-authority boundaries. "One Complete Journey" and full ecosystem language may overstate completeness.
- Correction needs: Add or strengthen public-preview/review-bounded labels, remove or protect sensitive personal address details, clarify that learning modules are not professional/legal/religious authority, and avoid "complete" language unless evidence and approval support it.
- Audit decision for this domain: Partially aligned; corrections required.

### C. `meuus.app`

- Intended role: Act / future guided journeys and tools.
- Observed current status: Public URL redirects to `https://www.meuus.app/` and displays app-like dashboard, account/profile/sign-out navigation, DLAS assessment calls, journey progress, tiers, purchases, prices, AI match score language, profile fields, member-code/tier language, and upgrade/purchase prompts.
- Current claims observed: Account, profile, notifications, sign out, dashboard, DLAS assessment, Life Score, Journey Day, AI match score, tier pricing, purchase calls, upgrade tier, member code, phone/city profile fields, and paid package language.
- App/account/AI/DLAS/payment/certification risk: High. Current visible claims may lead users to believe app accounts, DLAS assessment, AI scoring/matching, payments, tiers, and profile/account flows are live.
- Correction needs: Add immediate status boundaries or hold/replace live-product wording; clarify future/prototype status; remove or disable purchase/payment/account implications unless separately approved; ensure no user data collection is implied without approval; align with Phase One truth that AI/DLAS/accounts/payments/certification are not live.
- Audit decision for this domain: Misaligned for current governance; correction required before any public announcement or production-ready claim.

### D. `rujbel.org`

- Intended role: Founder / Human / Origin / Living Archive.
- Observed current status: rujbel.org production is not verified in this audit. The current available founder preview/source candidate is `https://ruju-founder-visions.lovable.app/about`.
- Lovable preview/source status: Preview page presents Md. Mahady Hasan / Ruju, founder/learner framing, values, founder perspective, pending verified background details, pending social/email/writing links, and an anti-overclaim boundary that does not claim medical, legal, psychological, employment, investment, partnership, office, award, or institutional support unless confirmed.
- Founder/institution separation: Mostly aligned in preview. The preview says meUus is founder-led and in-development and does not present the founder archive as meUus institutional authority.
- Sensitive data risks: Medium. The preview itself is cautious, but future rujbel.org work must avoid private founder/family/contact data, raw CV exposure, private archive data, and unverified credential or institutional claims.
- Production status risk: High if the Lovable preview is called production or treated as complete. Governance says it is a preview/source candidate only.
- Correction needs: Verify rujbel.org DNS/hosting/production path later, keep Lovable preview labeled as source candidate, finalize sensitive data policy, evidence labels, contact policy, and founder/institution separation before publication.
- Audit decision for this domain: Mostly aligned as preview/source; production status unknown and governed-incomplete.
