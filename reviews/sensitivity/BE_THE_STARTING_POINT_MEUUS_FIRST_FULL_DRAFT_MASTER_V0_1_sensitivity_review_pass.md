# Sensitivity Review Pass

## Governance Boundary

This sensitivity review pass does not approve publication.

This sensitivity review pass does not make the manuscript canonical.

This sensitivity review pass does not amend Frozen governance.

This sensitivity review pass does not rewrite the manuscript.

This file only identifies sensitivity categories, review needs, protection rules, and public-use constraints.

Any public use still requires editorial review, evidence review, public-expression review under Document 06, and canonical/provenance control under Document 07.

## Sensitivity Review Principles

- Protect dignity before expression.
- Preserve founder voice without exposing unsafe private material.
- Private pain is not public proof.
- Family and private-person references require consent-safe handling.
- Addiction/recovery language must not imply treatment, rehab, or clinical support.
- Mental-health language must not imply diagnosis, therapy, or professional care.
- Legal language must remain literacy-oriented and must not imply advice or representation.
- Religious/spiritual language must remain reflective and must not imply religious authority, fatwa, or spiritual judgment.
- Future-system language must not make AI, DLAS, Journey, Soul, paid levels, roles, services, or app systems appear live.
- Public trust language must not exceed evidence or operational capacity.
- Bangladesh/national-scale language must remain vision/context unless impact evidence exists.
- Claim <= Evidence remains active for every sensitive claim.

## Required Qualified Review Areas

Qualified or specialized review may be needed before public use for:

- therapy, diagnosis, treatment, recovery, addiction, or mental-health-adjacent material
- legal advice, legal support, legal representation, or dispute-related material
- religious authority, Quran/Seerah-related interpretation, spiritual judgment, or fatwa-like material
- AI/DLAS/assessment authority or validation claims
- payment, service availability, role/reward, or subscription claims
- claims involving identifiable private people or family members
- national-scale, public safety, public trust, or public impact claims

## Public Handling Guide

### Usually Safe After Light Edit

- Claim <= Evidence
- Birth is not completion
- Public foundation is not full platform
- Prototype is not production
- Research blueprint is not proof
- Correction as learning
- Boundaries protect the vision

### Safe Only With Label/Disclaimer

- Day One was birth
- Day Two is listening
- One Word Start
- Journey as reflection path
- Soul as learning/reflection layer
- Nine Pillars as directions
- Bangladesh/future-generation vision language

### Requires Heavy Rewrite or Abstraction

- founder pain
- founder overload
- founder as Client #001
- addiction/recovery-specific history
- family sacrifice references
- raw correction moments
- legal/professional-sensitive passages
- spiritual guilt or authority-sounding passages

### Internal or Private by Default

- identifiable family/private-person details
- detailed founder private history
- sensitive recovery details
- raw internal correction history
- unresolved legal/professional-sensitive material
- future-system descriptions that sound live
- role/payment/service wording

## Sensitivity Rewrite Patterns

Instead of:

"The founder proves the system works."

Use:

"The founder's experience revealed a need; it does not prove system effectiveness."

Instead of:

"meUus helps people recover."

Use:

"meUus may include educational and reflective material related to recovery themes, not treatment or professional care."

Instead of:

"The app guides users with AI."

Use:

"AI-assisted reflection remains a future direction unless separately verified and approved."

Instead of:

"DLAS analyzes life."

Use:

"DLAS remains a developing reflection and research concept, not a validated assessment system."

Instead of:

"Legal support."

Use:

"General legal literacy and preparation guidance, not legal advice or representation."

Instead of:

"Quran Journey teaches what faith means."

Use:

"Quran-related reflection or learning material requires careful provenance, humility, and religious authority boundaries."
