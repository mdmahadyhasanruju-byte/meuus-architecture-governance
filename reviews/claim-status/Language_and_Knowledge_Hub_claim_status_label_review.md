## 7. Public Page Rules

Before any public page is created, it must include:

- page status label
- content level label
- source/provenance status
- review status
- disclaimer where needed
- no guarantee language
- no religious authority claim
- no professional advice claim
- no certification claim unless verified
- no AI feature claim unless implemented
- no paid access claim unless backend/payment/account path exists
- no service availability claim unless operational evidence exists

## 8. High-Risk Content Controls

### A. Religious-Boundary Controls

- Quran/Seerah vocabulary must be language-learning support only.
- No tafsir/fatwa/ruling language.
- Use "requires religious-boundary review."

### B. Legal Controls

- Legal Hub content educational only.
- No case-specific advice unless verified lawyer engagement exists.
- Use "not legal advice."

### C. Mental Health/Recovery Controls

- Mind Beautifier/EQ/Drug Buster content must not replace professional care.
- Crisis/emergency routing must be clear before any public high-risk support claim.
- Use "not therapy or treatment."

### D. Financial/Earning Controls

- How to Earn content must not guarantee income.
- Investing/passive income requires financial risk disclaimer.
- Use "not financial advice" and "not income guarantee."

### E. Technical/Product Controls

- AI, DLAS, accounts, paid levels, email analysis, certification, dashboards, and app features must be marked Future/Prototype unless evidence exists.
