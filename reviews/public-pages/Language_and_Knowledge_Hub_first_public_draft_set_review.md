## 5. Universal Safety Findings

### Status/Authority Safety

The draft set consistently marks pages as Draft Public Preview Candidate, Level 00 Public Preview, requires review before public use, not published, and not production-ready. The source/provenance line also states the material is not canonical. The main improvement is to make Canonical: Not Canonical, Runtime: Not Live, Deployment: Not Approved, and Implementation authority: None explicit in every page-level status block before public review.

### Claim/Evidence Safety

Claim <= Evidence is preserved. The drafts use learning support, draft, public-preview, and future/review-needed language. No page presents vocabulary as verified, curriculum as final, or systems as live.

### Runtime/Feature Safety

No draft implies live AI, DLAS, accounts, dashboards, payments, subscriptions, certification, cloud storage, or automated decisions. The Daily Learning Log and Error Log Template are framed as personal/manual exercises.

### Human Safety

The drafts avoid diagnosis, therapy, emergency support, legal advice, financial advice, religious ruling, and recovery service language. The shared disclaimer is present across the draft pages.

### Language/Vocabulary Safety

The draft set avoids unreviewed Arabic claims except where multilingual terms are listed in the index or title contexts. The home page correctly labels Arabic Path as future/review-needed. Concept pages using Bangla/Arabic terms should remain HOLD for public publication until linguistic and boundary review, but are safe for next internal revision.

### Copy/Tone Safety

The tone is plain, humble, educational, and non-sales-heavy. CTAs are limited to "Continue to Deeper Study" and do not imply paid, AI, legal, clinical, or live feature access.

### Disclaimer Safety

The shared disclaimer is present and broad enough for this first draft set. It clearly states the pages are not final curriculum, not professional advice, not diagnosis, not legal advice, not religious ruling, not financial advice, not therapy, not emergency support, and not proof that advanced meUus systems are live.

### Public Status Consistency

The drafts are consistent with the current non-public, non-canonical, non-production status. They do not contradict the Language & Knowledge Hub review controls.
