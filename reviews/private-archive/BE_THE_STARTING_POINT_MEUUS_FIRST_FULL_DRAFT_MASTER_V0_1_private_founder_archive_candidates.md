# Private/Founder Archive Candidate List

## Governance Boundary

This file does not approve publication.

This file does not make private material canonical.

This file does not amend Frozen governance.

This file only identifies private/archive-sensitive themes for protection.

Any later public use requires editorial review, evidence review, sensitivity review, and public-expression review under Documents 05, 06, and 07.

## Private/Archive Candidate Categories

### A. Founder Personal History Material

Includes:

- personal struggle
- recovery references
- founder pain
- founder emotional state
- founder as Client #001
- founder overload
- founder private becoming

Treatment:

Private/founder archive by default; public only after heavy editorial abstraction.

### B. Family/Private-Person References

Includes:

- mother, father, brother, sister
- family sacrifice
- private family memory
- identifiable private people

Treatment:

Private/archive by default unless consent-safe, anonymized, and necessary.

### C. Addiction/Recovery-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- addiction history references
- recovery experience
- rehab/recovery implications
- Drug Buster adjacency

Treatment:

Private/archive by default unless rewritten as general educational principle with professional-care boundary.

### D. Emotional/Mental-Health-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- emotional distress
- fear
- shame
- anger
- instability
- mental health references

Treatment:

Use public only with educational/reflection framing; avoid therapy/diagnosis implication.

### E. Religious/Spiritual-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- Bismillah, Alhamdulillah, InShaAllah
- Quran Journey
- Seerah Journey
- spiritual guilt
- faith and responsibility

Treatment:

Public reflection possible, but avoid religious authority, fatwa, spiritual judgment, or manipulation.

### F. Legal/Professional-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- legal literacy
- legal hub
- disputes
- public/legal responsibility

Treatment:

Public only as general education/literacy; no advice or representation.

### G. Founder-System Identity Risk

Includes:

- meUus too closely identified with founder pain
- founder experience universalized
- founder story treated as proof

Treatment:

Keep private or rewrite to distinguish origin narrative from validated evidence.

### H. Public-System Overclaim-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- full app
- AI
- DLAS
- Journey
- Soul
- paid levels
- roles/rewards
- services
- public trust/safety language

Treatment:

Private/archive unless status-labeled and evidence-checked.

### I. Bangladesh/National-Scale-Sensitive Material

Includes:

- Bangladesh
- future generations
- national transformation
- social impact

Treatment:

Public only as vision/context; avoid impact claims without evidence.

### J. Raw Correction/History Material

Includes:

- "abar daw" misunderstanding
- screenshot correction
- command mode pause
- assistant/founder correction moments

Treatment:

Archive by default; public only after anonymized principle-level rewriting.

### K. Internal Governance-Adjacent Material

Includes:

- Source-of-Truth internal rules
- claim register logic
- governance terms
- frozen document references

Treatment:

Keep as internal reference unless converted into simplified public explanation.

## Public Abstraction Guidance

- Convert personal pain into principle only when necessary.
- Remove or anonymize identifiable private-person details.
- Do not quote family/private-person references directly.
- Do not use addiction/recovery history as marketing proof.
- Do not use founder pain to validate system effectiveness.
- Do not turn spiritual language into authority.
- Do not turn legal or therapy-related language into professional advice.
- Do not expose raw correction moments unless they are rewritten as general learning principles.
- Prefer "origin insight" over "personal proof."
- Prefer "the founder's experience revealed a need" over "the founder proves the system works."
