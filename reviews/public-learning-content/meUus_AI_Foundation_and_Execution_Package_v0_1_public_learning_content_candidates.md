## Public Educational Safety Rules

- Explain AI as functional capability, not human soul/consciousness.
- Separate scientific claims from philosophical/reflection framing.
- Do not state metaphysical claims as scientific proof.
- Do not guarantee learning outcomes.
- Do not imply meUus AI is live.
- Do not imply meYoo is live.
- Do not imply DLAS is validated.
- Do not imply local AI is automatically private or secure.
- Do not imply RAG guarantees truth.
- Do not imply AI replaces human professionals.
- Add disclaimers for legal, medical, therapy, recovery, religious, AI/DLAS, and personal-data themes.
- Use sources for technical/historical claims.
- Use status labels for meUus-specific future systems.
