## Purpose

Develop a conceptual taxonomy for evidence, status labels, and truth claims across the meUus ecosystem.

This Draft identifies the intended work without defining the full taxonomy. It is exploratory, is not canonical, and must not be cited as authority.

---

## Scope

This document is intended to clarify:

- Claim and evidence boundaries.
- Evidence types and limits.
- Status labels and verification levels.
- Truth-claim limits.
- Public and internal status language.
- Operational, canonical, runtime, and public-expression evidence.
- Outdated, conflicting, insufficient, and unverifiable evidence.
- Uncertainty and aspiration.
- Escalation before expression.

Detailed definitions and classifications remain to be developed in a later Working draft.

- Making the taxonomy too complex to apply consistently.
