## Severity Labels

| Severity | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Low | Low public-risk claim if accurately labeled and evidence-bounded |
| Medium | Requires careful wording, status label, or editorial review |
| High | Requires evidence check and formal review before public use |
| Critical | Must not be public or operationally used without explicit review, evidence, and authority boundary |

Use Critical for:

- therapy, diagnosis, treatment, recovery support, or clinical implications.
- legal advice or legal representation.
- religious authority.
- AI/DLAS assessment authority.
- payment or service availability.
- private family or private-person exposure.

## Required Review Buckets

### Public-safe after light edit

- `Claim <= Evidence`.
- Birth is not completion.
- Prototype is not production.
- Research blueprint is not proof.
- Public foundation is not full platform.
- Source-of-Truth as discipline.
- One Word Start as beginning doorway.

### Public-safe only with labels/disclaimers

- public foundation / launch language.
- future ecosystem language.
- Journey prototype language.
- Soul learning path language.
- Bangladesh/future-generation vision language.
- governance-adjacent book language.

### Needs evidence before public use

- live capability claims.
- full platform claims.
- AI/meYoo claims.
- DLAS/assessment claims.
- research/validation claims.
- public trust/safety claims.
- outcome/impact claims.
- partnership/endorsement claims.

### Internal/founder archive only

- founder personal-history material.
- family/private-person references.
- addiction/recovery autobiographical material.
- private source-of-truth correction episodes.
- sensitive archive material involving identifiable people.

### Do not publish unless rewritten

- payment/service availability language.
- legal support language.
- therapy/diagnosis/treatment language.
- recovery support language.
- religious authority language.
- AI/DLAS authority language.
- public claims that imply full platform launch.

### Requires qualified review

- religious/Seerah/Quran-related material.
- legal advice or legal representation implications.
- therapy, diagnosis, treatment, recovery, crisis, or emergency implications.
- professional advice implications.
- private-person references.
- sensitive user-data or privacy claims.

## Required Softening Language

Instead of:

```text
DLAS analyzes life
```

Use:

```text
DLAS is a developing reflection and research concept, not a validated assessment system.
```

Instead of:

```text
meUus app is live
```

Use:

```text
meUus currently has a public foundation and bounded prototypes; the full app remains future/planned unless otherwise evidenced.
```

Instead of:

```text
recovery support
```

Use:

```text
educational and reflective material related to recovery themes, not treatment or professional care.
```

Instead of:

```text
legal support
```

Use:

```text
general legal literacy and preparation guidance, not legal advice or representation.
```

Instead of:

```text
AI guidance
```

Use:

```text
future AI-assisted reflection direction, not a live AI authority or decision system.
```

Additional safe patterns:

| Instead of | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `validated assessment` | `planned assessment concept requiring validation before use` |
| `safe for users` | `designed with safety as a governance concern; operational safety requires evidence` |
| `changes lives` | `intended to support reflection and responsible next steps` |
| `spiritual guidance` | `spiritual reflection or learning material, not religious authority` |
| `national impact` | `long-term aspiration or context, not demonstrated impact` |
| `source-of-truth proves...` | `source material records a claim that requires review` |
