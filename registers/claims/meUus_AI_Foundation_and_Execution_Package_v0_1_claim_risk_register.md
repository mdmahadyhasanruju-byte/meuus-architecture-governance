## Required Softening Language

Instead of:

"AI understands."

Use:

"AI can process and generate language in ways that resemble understanding, but this does not prove human-like consciousness or lived understanding."

Instead of:

"meUus AI will guide users."

Use:

"Future meUus AI-assisted reflection may support users, but it is not currently approved as a live authority or decision system."

Instead of:

"DLAS analyzes life."

Use:

"DLAS is a developing reflection/research concept, not a validated diagnostic, scoring, or assessment system."

Instead of:

"RAG is the solution."

Use:

"RAG is a strong candidate architecture for grounded project knowledge, subject to technical, privacy, and evaluation review."

Instead of:

"Local AI keeps data safe."

Use:

"Local AI may reduce some data-sharing risks, but security still requires proper device, storage, access, and operational controls."

Instead of:

"This package can build meUus AI."

Use:

"This package is incoming source material for future AI learning and architecture review; it is not production implementation approval."
