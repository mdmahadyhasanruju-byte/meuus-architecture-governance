## Why Correction Matters

Correction protects learning. A mistake can show where understanding was incomplete. When the learner records the correction, the mistake becomes part of the learning path.

## Common Misunderstanding

Mistake is not failure. A mistake can become useful when it is noticed, corrected, and practiced again.

## Reflection Question

What mistake helped me understand something more clearly?

## One Small Action

Choose one recent confusion. Write what you thought, what changed, and what you will practice next.
