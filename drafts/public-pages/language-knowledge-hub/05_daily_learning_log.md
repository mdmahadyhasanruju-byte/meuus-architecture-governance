## How To Use

Use this as a short daily note. Keep it simple. The goal is not to prove mastery. The goal is to notice what changed, what remains unclear, and what small practice can come next.

## Common Misunderstanding

A learning log is not proof that a learner has mastered a subject. It is a record of attention, effort, questions, and next steps.
