## 2. Governance Boundary

- This checklist does not approve publication.
- This checklist does not approve implementation.
- This checklist does not approve deployment.
- This checklist does not make draft pages canonical.
- This checklist does not verify vocabulary.
- This checklist does not approve Arabic/Quran/Seerah authority.
- This checklist does not approve legal, financial, psychological, medical, recovery, religious, or scientific claims.
- This checklist only reviews whether draft candidate pages are safe enough to move toward next internal revision.

Core rule:
A draft page may only move forward if it preserves Claim <= Evidence, carries correct status labels, avoids authority claims, avoids runtime overclaims, includes required disclaimers, and does not imply live features or professional services.

## 6. GO / HOLD / BLOCK Model

### GO — Ready for Next Internal Revision Only

Allowed when:
- labels complete
- disclaimer complete
- no high-risk claims
- no live/runtime implication
- no professional/religious/legal/financial/therapy authority
- no specialist review required before internal revision

### HOLD — Needs Correction Before Next Stage

Use when:
- wording is unclear
- labels incomplete
- disclaimer missing
- claim stronger than evidence
- future feature could be misunderstood as live
- vocabulary/Arabic/legal/EQ/earning content needs review

### BLOCK — Not Safe to Move Forward

Use when:
- page implies live AI/DLAS/accounts/payments/certification
- page gives legal, financial, therapy, diagnosis, religious ruling, or recovery service impression
- page contains private/internal founder material without approval
- page contradicts current public status boundaries
- page creates public trust risk or user safety risk
