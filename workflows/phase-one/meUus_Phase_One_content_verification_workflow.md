## 2. Purpose

This workflow defines how meUus content must move from source/draft material into verified, labeled, review-bounded, and public-safe content before any public use or announcement.

It exists to protect:
- Claim <= Evidence
- public truth
- founder privacy
- user safety
- learning integrity
- religious/legal/professional boundaries
- no overclaiming
- no accidental production/public authority claims

## 3. Core Workflow Rule

NO CONTENT MOVES TO PUBLIC USE UNTIL IT HAS A SOURCE RECORD, EVIDENCE LABEL, PRIVACY/SAFETY REVIEW, AUTHORITY BOUNDARY REVIEW, AND FOUNDER APPROVAL WHERE REQUIRED.

## 4. Content Lifecycle Stages

| Stage | Purpose | Required artifact | Allowed status | Blocker conditions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Stage 0 - Raw source / memory / idea | Capture unprocessed material without treating it as public truth. | Raw note, source capture, or memory note | Internal only, Draft | Public use, public claim, or external sharing before intake |
| Stage 1 - Intake record | Create a traceable entry for the content item. | Intake record | Internal only, Draft | No owner, no source context, or unclear content boundary |
| Stage 2 - Source classification | Identify whether the source is public, private, self-reported, document-supported, or unknown. | Source classification record | Internal only, Draft, Needs review | Unknown source treated as verified or public-ready |
| Stage 3 - Evidence labeling | Assign the correct evidence label before rewriting or publication review. | Evidence label record | Draft, Review-bounded | No evidence label or label stronger than evidence supports |
| Stage 4 - Privacy and sensitive data review | Check founder, family, contact, recovery, user-data, and third-party risks. | Privacy/safety review | Internal only, Restricted, Review-bounded | Sensitive data without deliberate review and Founder approval |
| Stage 5 - Authority boundary review | Check legal, religious, medical, psychological, professional, AI, DLAS, and app/service implications. | Authority boundary review | Review-bounded | Advice, ruling, diagnosis, guarantee, or live-service implication without approval |
| Stage 6 - Public-safe rewrite | Rewrite content so it is honest, bounded, dignified, and safe for the intended domain. | Public-safe rewrite | Draft, Public-preview candidate | Raw private material, overclaiming, or missing disclaimer remains |
| Stage 7 - Founder review | Confirm the wording, evidence label, privacy status, and boundary fit. | Founder review note or approval record | Review-bounded, Held | Founder approval required but not recorded |
| Stage 8 - Public-preview candidate | Prepare content for controlled preview without public announcement. | Public-preview candidate record | Public-preview, Held | Announcement, indexing, canonical, or production-ready claim before approval |
| Stage 9 - Public-preview verification | Verify route/page rendering, labels, disclaimers, privacy, and no unintended functionality. | Verification report | Public-preview, Review-bounded | Missing safety blocks, unexpected forms, inputs, login, payment, or live-service cues |
| Stage 10 - Public use decision | Decide whether content remains held, enters public-preview, or is approved for limited public use. | Public use decision record | Held, Public-preview, Approved limited use | Publication decision missing or announcement hold still active |
| Stage 11 - Published/public route status record if approved | Record public route status only if publication/public use is separately approved. | Published/public route status record | Public foundation, Live only if verified and approved | Publication, indexing, canonical, or production-ready claims without separate gate |
| Stage 12 - Periodic review and correction | Re-check content as evidence, risks, domains, or governance status changes. | Periodic review or correction backlog | Review-bounded, Correction required, Held | Outdated claims, changed status, broken boundaries, or unresolved corrections |

## 8. Authority Boundary Review

### Quran/Seerah

- educational/reflection only
- not fatwa
- not tafsir authority
- review required before public expansion

### Legal/Law

- educational only
- not legal advice
- official sources and legal review required before stronger claims

### Emotional Intelligence / Recovery

- reflection/education only
- not therapy/diagnosis
- crisis/emergency boundary required if relevant

### Professional / Employment

- educational only
- no job/employment guarantee

### AI/DLAS/App

- future/prototype unless separately approved
- no runtime/service claim without evidence

## 10. Blocker Conditions

The following conditions block public use:
- sensitive founder/contact/family data without approval
- user data collection implication without privacy approval
- payment/account/AI/DLAS live claim without approval
- legal/religious/medical/psychological authority claim
- "complete/verified/official/production-ready" claim without evidence
- raw private screenshots/messages
- third-party private data without consent
- public announcement before hold release
